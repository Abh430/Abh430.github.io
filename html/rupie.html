Introducing the

Rupie Network

Rupie is a gaming community built from the ground up to provide powerful new ways for gamers and game developers to build games together.

As a Gamer

Influence games as they are being created. Contribute assets and get compensated securely for your work. Engage directly with developers and hold them accountable to their milestones.

As a Developer

Give your fans a voice by giving them suggestion & voting powers. Commission + rent digital assets from the maker community. Keep communities engaged with incentives and exclusive announcements.

It's time we give the power back to those who play & make games.

Collaborative Game Creation

Build with transparency & accountability.

Together we can fix the distrust associated with early access and leverage community talent to more reliably and quickly forge the games we love playing.

With Rupie's community engagement features, developers no longer have to wonder whether features should be built. Gamers vote on development milestones: Should we include this new map? Should we introduce this new mechanic? Does this weapon need to be nerfed?

Through transparent, deliverable milestones, early access developers are able to get partial funding based on meeting certain criteria — essentially escrow tiers. Didn't finish your game? You don’t get paid.

Digital Asset Marketplace

Creators contribute. Developers reward. Everyone wins.

Are you a creator with 3D models, storylines, and other creative lying around from an old game you sunset? With Asset Rentals easily form smart contracts with developers to breathe new life into latent digital assets and secure blockchain-powered attribution for your original work.

As a developer, browse through a large list of assets in a rich interface to find that perfect script, model, or audio file for your game. Can’t find what you need? Commission custom assets through the Rupie Network.
